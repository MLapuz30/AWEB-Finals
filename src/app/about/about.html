About ResQ

Emergency Response, Reimagined

ResQ is a barangay-level emergency hotline directory system designed to provide verified, real-time access to life-saving services when every second counts.

40% Faster Response
100% Verified Data
1000+ Users Served

Our Mission

Traditional emergency contact methods—like tarpaulin banners and social media posts—are often outdated, inaccurate, and hard to access during critical moments. ResQ solves this problem by providing a centralized, digital platform where residents can instantly find and contact verified emergency services.

We empower communities with the tools they need to respond faster, communicate better, and ultimately save more lives.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Safety First

Our primary mission is to ensure every resident has immediate access to life-saving emergency services.

Accuracy Matters

All contact information is verified and regularly updated by authorized barangay officials.

Continuous Improvement

We constantly evolve based on community feedback to better serve emergency response needs.

Built by Students, For Communities
